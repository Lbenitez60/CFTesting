My Experience in Tennis


I have played tennis since age 4. I really enjoy tennis and I try to play as much as possible. I used to play more than 3 times per week but now because of time constraints I only play 1 time per week. I used to play at the USTA Chase Center, which is next to the Arthur Ashe stadium, the biggest tennis stadium in the world. Now I play at the Roosevelt Island Racquet Club, because the commute is way more convenient. Images of these places is below.
